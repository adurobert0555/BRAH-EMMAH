Paediatric

“Provides medical care and treatment to infants, children, and adolescents, addressing various health issues, such as infections, developmental problems, and chronic conditions.”

Some potential symptoms:

  • Fever
  • Vomiting or diarrhea
  • Coughing or difficulty breathing
  • Rash or skin infections
  • Developmental delays or concerns

X-Ray

“Uses imaging technologies like X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs to diagnose and monitor medical conditions, such as fractures, tumors, and internal injuries.”

Some potential symptoms associated with diseases related to X-Ray

  • Fractures: Pain, swelling, or deformity
  • Lung diseases: Coughing, shortness of breath, or chest pain
  • Tumors: Various symptoms depending on location and type

Dental

“Provides diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of dental and oral health issues, such as cavities, gum disease, and tooth extractions.”

Some potential symptoms associated with diseases related to Dental

  • Toothache or pain
  • Sensitivity to hot or cold temperatures
  • Bleeding gums
  • Bad breath
  • Swollen or tender gums

Ophthalmology

“Diagnoses and treats eye disorders and diseases, including cataracts, glaucoma, and vision problems.”

Some potential symptoms associated with diseases related to Eye

  • Double vision
  • Eye pain or discomfort
  • Redness or swelling of the eyes.
  • Sensitivity to light

Antenatal

“Provides medical care and monitoring to pregnant women, ensuring a healthy pregnancy and addressing any complications that may arise.”

Potential symptoms that may occur during Pregnancy

  • Vaginal bleeding or spotting.
  • Severe morning sickness.
  • Swelling or sudden weight gain.
  • Back pain or pelvic pressure
  • Decreased fetal movement

E.C.G

“Conducts tests to measure the electrical activity of the heart, helping diagnose heart conditions, such as arrhythmias, coronary artery disease, and cardiac abnormalities.”

Some potential symptoms that qualifies you to take an E.C.G. scan

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Palpitations or irregular heartbeat
